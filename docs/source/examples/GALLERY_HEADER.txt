Examples
================

This gallery contains categorized examples and tutorials for the **Infinity Tomographic Reconstruction** project.

Explore workflows including device creation, data preparation, and reconstruction techniques.

Sections:

- **EasyPETCT**: PET/CT device workflows and reconstructions
- **SiemensIntevoBoldSPECTCT**: Different scanner geometry demonstrations
- **iPET**: SPECT/CT device workflows and reconstructions
